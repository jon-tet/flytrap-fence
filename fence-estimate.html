What Affects Your Fence Cost?

Fence Length & Height

More linear feet = more materials and labor. Taller fences (8 ft) cost more than standard 6 ft privacy fences.

Terrain & Slopes

Flat yards are easier. Slopes, rocks, or roots may require extra work to ensure your fence is level and secure.

Gates & Access

Single walk gates, double drive gates, and custom hardware add to the total. We'll help you plan the right access points.

Removal of Old Fence

Need an old fence torn out? We handle demolition and disposal for an additional fee. Just ask for a removal quote.

Style & Upgrades

Board-on-board, cap & trim, decorative post caps—premium styles and finishes look great but cost a bit more.

Property Location

Most of Wilmington, Leland, and Hampstead are in our core service area. Further locations may have a travel fee.
